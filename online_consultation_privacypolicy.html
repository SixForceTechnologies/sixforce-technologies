Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 20, 2026

Welcome to Dr. Chaturanana's Homoeopathy (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal and medical information is handled with the highest level of care and security.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application and related medical consultation services (the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

To provide professional medical consultations, we collect several types of information:

  • Personal Identification: Name, age, gender, phone number, and physical address (for medicine delivery).
  • Medical Records: Clinical history, chief complaints, past history, family history, and prescriptions filled by our doctors.
  • Media & Files: Medical reports, images, or documents you choose to upload to your profile for analysis.
  • Technical Data: Device model, OS version, and unique identifiers required to maintain session stability.

2. App Permissions & Data Usage

The App requires specific permissions to function effectively. We only use these for the purposes stated below:

  • Camera & Microphone: Essential for conducting secure video and audio consultations between the doctor and patient.
  • Storage (Photos & Media): Used only when you choose to upload medical records or download prescriptions provided by the clinic.
  • Internet: Required to sync your appointments and records with our secure Firebase and Agora cloud infrastructure.

3. How We Use Information

Your data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate medical consultations and professional evaluation.
  • To maintain your cumulative medical history for long-term treatment tracking.
  • To process medicine orders and ensure delivery to your specific address.
  • To provide clinical staff and pharmacies with the necessary details to fulfill your prescriptions.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal or medical data. We only share information with trusted service providers necessary for our operations:

  • Google Firebase: For secure database hosting and user authentication.
  • Agora.io: To provide high-quality, encrypted video calling infrastructure.
  • Authorized Pharmacies: Shared only when a doctor specifically forwards a prescription to a pharmacy at your request.

5. Secure Record Keeping & Consultations

As per our clinical terms, consultations may be recorded for security, quality assurance, and documentation purposes. These recordings are stored securely and remain strictly confidential, accessible only to required medical staff under legal necessity.

6. Data Retention & User Rights

We retain your medical records as long as your treatment is active or as required by healthcare regulations.

Your Rights: You have the right to access your medical records, request corrections to inaccurate information, or request the deletion of your account. Note that clinical records may still be retained if required for legal or regulatory medical documentation.

7. Security Measures

We implement robust administrative and technical security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Emergency Disclaimer

This App is NOT intended for emergency use. We do not provide emergency services. In case of a medical emergency, please visit the nearest hospital or healthcare provider immediately.
